The Power Detector

What is the Smart Power Detector

The Smart Power Detector is a Senior Design Project from the University of Texas at Austin, sponsored by Boeing. The project aims to use non-intrusive load monitoring (NILM) and machine learning models in order distinguish individual devices from an aggregate signal. The team collected data via an emonPi, and used it both for training as well as testing purposes. Indivudual power profiles of devices were determined via the emonPi and used to train the macchine learning model. Once the model was trained, real time data from the emonPi was fed to the machine learning model, to determine if the model could accurately determine which device was plugged in.

Who are we?

We are a group of seniors from the University of Texas at Austin. This project was completed as a part of our Senior Design course, and was sponsored by the CASA team at Boeing.
